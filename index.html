About Us

The Dog's Bollocks at YARD is where bold flavors, unforgettable experiences, and a welcoming vibe come together. Nestled in the heart of Cape Town, we’re not just a restaurant; we’re a celebration of deliciously crafted dishes and good times with great company.

  • Known for our indulgent, gourmet burgers made from the finest local ingredients
  • A laid-back atmosphere where everyone feels like part of the family
  • A diverse menu that caters to all tastes, from meat lovers to plant-based enthusiasts

Whether you're looking for a casual bite or a lively night out, The Dog's Bollocks at YARD is the place to be for food, fun, and great memories.
